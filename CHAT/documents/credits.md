4C uses the crypto library that is part of the
OpenSSL package from
[The OpenSSL Project](https://www.openssl.org)
Parts of the software used was developed by Eric Young and he
owns the copyright to it.
[View OpenSSL and SSLEay Licenses]({{< ref "ssllicense.md" >}})
The following books were helpful in understanding
the complexities and issues involved in writing secure
software and the issues involved in encryption algorithms.

- Applied Cryptography by Bruce Schneier - This is the bible of cryptographic algorithms.
- SSH The Secure Shell, The definitive guide from O'Reilly - This book was really helpful in that a lot of the issues that 4C needed to addresss are also addressed by SSH.
- Network Security with OpenSSL, also from O'Reilly.
- Crypto by Steven Levy - This book was an enjoyable read and gives a good history of cryptography. This book, like any book by Steven Levy is very worthwhile reading.
